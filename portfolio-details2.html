DISCUSSION

The human body remains imperfect despite the wonders of artificial intelligence. Researchers are working to find solutions to those problems. A.I. has the power to strengthen, increase, and make up for the shortcomings in our bodies. Individual heterogeneity was a particularly difficult problem since it made machine learning approaches incapable of detecting trends. But gradually, a kind of AI known as deep learning became sensitive enough to differentiate between voice and speech data. Despite the fact that deep learning algorithms required a large amount of training data, they may eventually learn to detect words regardless of accents and other variances in speech patterns. Deep learning is being used by many companies, most notably the San Francisco-based startup Enlitic, in the medical industry to identify suspicious tumors.
